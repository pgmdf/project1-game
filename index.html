Future Transport Quartet/Card game

If it comes to getting from A to B in an urban environment you can luckily choose from a great variety. Some of the options are transferred to playing cards - with all their pros and cons.
What do you think is the striking criteria of each card? Will it beat the one of your opponent aka the computer?

  1. Pick a criteria and play that value
  2. Choose wisely and collect as many points
  3. Try to beat the computer
  4. Good luck!

Keyvalues like velocity and energy are based on research at the below listed sources, but also on estimaes due to a lack of convinient sources. The energy is based on a person weighing 70kg.
Fun factor, flexibility and load capacity are estimates.
